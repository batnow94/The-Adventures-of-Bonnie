Time Control
Lets Bonnie do things such as rewind time, go into the future, among other things.

Creates playable clones of Bonnie.

Control The Earth
Change the games map.

Power Absorb
Absorb powers from others.

Possession
Take control of someone else.

Teleportation
Able to teleport anywhere.

Advanced Healing
Heal at an exceptional rate.

Summon Weapons
Is able to summon weapons into his hand at any time.
